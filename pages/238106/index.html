# OA研究课题

# 总体了解

Simply put, office automation is a tool or technology that makes a process more efficient and less labor-intensive.

Office automation should offer your employees both flexibility and reliability in supporting their daily tasks.

  1. Easy workflow designer 协同办公 流程 跟踪
  2. Mobile compatibility 移动办公 Technology has brought the concept of work outside of the office. What this means is that work isn't somewhere you go — it's something you do.
  3. Integration with other software 应用集成
  4. Reports and analytics 报告分析及决策
  5. Access control options 权限控制 每个人可以定义自己的工作空间
  6. 数据电子化 数据电子化，资源、信息、知识、文档 已有的、新挖掘的 信息存储、数据交换和数据管理 表单 博客编写、预览、查看、搜索等功能 社区、问答等功能 在线网盘等功能

8MSaaS功能： automatically connects to CRM, HCM and PM enabling the underlying data to interlink with each other

Traditional OA includes document, workflow and collaboration management and some other functions to improve usability. 8MSaaS is a new generation, advanced OA application which automatically connects to CRM, HCM and PM enabling the underlying data to interlink with each other. 8MSaaS not only provides assistance in managing processes, communication and team work for new employees, it also helps experienced ones in tracking, analyzing data, in report generation and extracting business intelligence. 8MSaaS OA allows both new employees and experienced ones to customize different menus and interfaces.
